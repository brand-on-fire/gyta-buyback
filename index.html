What is Gyta Buyback?

Gyta Buyback is a WordPress plugin that extends WooCommerce’s functionality. WooCommerce is great at handling normal eCommerce shop functionality where the customer is buying something from the shop owner.

But, for Trade-in and Buyback businesses, the process is reversed! We’ve optimized the process when the customer is selling something to the shop owner. 

How does Gyta Buyback work?

Gyta Buyback changes how WooCommerce works. It adds new payment methods that are reversed, so the WooCommerce Store pays the customer instead of the customer paying the Store. To facilitate payment, customer payout details are collected on checkout. 

Through EasyPost integration, shipping labels can be automatically generate so your customers can easily send in their products. 

Is Gyta Buyback Secure?

Absolutely! All information about your business is stored on your web server. Gyta Buyback does not make “calls home” or have backdoors into your installation. The only 3rd party connection is via EasyPost API, which requires customer address details to generate shipping labels. 

Your data stays with you and your customers are protected.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I set up any kind of product?

Yes! You can define your products, categories, attributes, variations, etc., any way you want.

What if I need unique pricing for product variations?

No problem! You can set your variations and product attributes any way you want in WooCommerce.

How are orders handled?

Gyta Buyback extends WooCommerce, which is the most popular cart software for WordPress. It shows your orders in your WordPress administration screen, and you can manage orders there.

How are products shipped?

Gyta is integrated with EasyPost. You can set up EasyPost to use USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL, RoyalPost, CanadaPost, and a whole array of other shipping carriers.

 

Which payment methods are supported?

Gyta supports all major buyback payment methods. PayPal, check, Venmo, Zelle, and Interac e-Transfer are all currently supported. You can choose which payment methods you will support.

Can I change the look and feel?

Absolutely! Gyta works within WooCommerce and WordPress. These are highly-customizable products. Many changes you can make yourself. There’s a zillion themes in the marketplace to choose from (both paid and free), or you can always bring on your own custom designer.

What themes can I use?

You can use any theme! I do suggest a theme that has been developed for use by WooCommerce, but that’s not a requirement.

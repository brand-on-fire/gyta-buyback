What is Gyta Buyback?#

Gyta Buyback is a WordPress plugin that extends WooCommerce with fully-featured Buyback and Trade-in capabilities. WooCommerce handles normal eCommerce where the customer buys from the store — Gyta Buyback reverses the process so the store purchases products from the customer.

Whether you run a phone buyback operation, an electronics trade-in program, or any product repurchase business, Gyta Buyback gives you everything you need out of the box.

How Does It Work?#

Gyta Buyback changes how WooCommerce works in three key ways:

  1. Reversed payment flow — New payment methods where the store pays the customer (PayPal, Venmo, Zelle, Check, Amazon Gift Card, Interac eTransfer, Crypto/Stablecoin).
  2. Payout details collection — Customer payout information is collected at checkout.
  3. Automated shipping labels — Through EasyPost integration, shipping labels are automatically generated so customers can send in their products.

All core features are free. Download Gyta Buyback from WordPress.org. PRO and Business plans unlock additional payment methods, admin features, and premium support.

Prerequisites#

Before installing Gyta Buyback, you need:

  • WordPress (self-hosted, PHP 7.4+, MySQL 5.7+, HTTPS)
  • WooCommerce 5.0+ installed and activated
  • EasyPost account (optional, for shipping labels)

Is Gyta Buyback Secure?#

Absolutely. All data is stored on your web server. Gyta Buyback does not make "calls home" or have backdoors. The only third-party connection is EasyPost for shipping labels.

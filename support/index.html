How can I offer standard buying and buyback / trade-in on the same site?

You could check out the Payment Methods by Product & Country for WooCommerce plugin, which forces different payment methods for different products. Set all of your buyback products to payment methods offered by Gyta BuyBack and set all normal buy products to your other preferred payment methods.

Why does the checkout say, “There are no payment methods available”?

WooCommerce offers several different checkout blocks. Gyta Buyback is not compatible with all of them. You’ll need to change to a ‘standard’ checkout block so additional payment methods will be supported.

Edit your checkout page and look for a button to “Switch to classic checkout”. That should take care of the issue. You can also read more about it here: https://woo.com/document/cart-checkout-blocks-status/
